01 · Scope of Work

Apollo Advancement will conduct a comprehensive development audit of Maryvale Academy covering seven areas: organizational culture, board engagement, donor data and systems, communications and storytelling, fundraising programs, donor stewardship, and capital campaign readiness.

The audit will be paired with a written 12-month strategic development plan: a month-by-month calendar of every fundraising activity, donor communication, and stewardship rhythm. The strategic plan is delivered as a starting point for discussion and refinement, not a final directive.

02 · Deliverables
01 Audit report A comprehensive document presenting findings across all seven audit areas, with specific observations, benchmarks, and prioritized recommendations specific to Maryvale Academy.
02 Strategic plan A 12-month development roadmap covering solicitations, communications, stewardship, and role assignments.
03 Board presentation A virtual, 60-90 minute presentation of findings and recommendations to the full board or a designated committee.
04 30-day follow-up One follow-up call approximately 30 days after the board presentation to answer questions and support early implementation.

03 · On-Site Visit

The engagement includes a two-day on-site visit to Ottawa for stakeholder interviews with board members, the executive director, and key staff; and a campus tour. Dates will be coordinated with Maryvale and scheduled within the first three weeks of the engagement.

04 · Timeline

Formal start date: May 19, 2026. Estimated completion: 6 to 8 weeks from receipt of the initial document package. A detailed document and data request will be issued upon signing of this letter.

06 · Payment Terms

Payment is due in two installments:

(a) First installment (50%): $4,691.76, due upon signing of this letter. This installment is non-refundable and compensates Apollo Advancement for reserving the engagement period, beginning document review and analysis, and the opportunity cost of declining other work during this time.

(b) Second installment (50%): $4,691.76, due upon delivery of the final written report.

Invoices will be issued by Apollo Advancement. Payment may be made by e-transfer to jjalsevac@gmail.com or by cheque.

07 · Cancellation

Either party may terminate this engagement with 7 days' written notice. In the event of cancellation by Maryvale, the first installment is non-refundable. If cancellation occurs after the on-site visit has taken place, both installments are due in full.

In the event of cancellation by Apollo Advancement, Apollo Advancement will refund any fees not yet earned on a pro-rata basis based on work completed to date.

08 · What Is Not Included

This engagement does not include implementation of audit recommendations, ongoing fundraising counsel, campaign management, or additional on-site visits beyond the two days specified. These services are available through Apollo Advancement and can be discussed following the audit.

09 · Confidentiality

All information provided by Maryvale Academy in connection with this engagement will be treated as confidential. Apollo Advancement will not disclose donor data, financial information, board discussions, or any other proprietary information to third parties without written consent from Maryvale. Maryvale's name and general engagement details may be referenced in Apollo Advancement's portfolio and marketing materials unless Maryvale requests otherwise in writing.

10 · Independent Contractor

Apollo Advancement is engaged as an independent contractor. Nothing in this agreement creates an employer-employee, partnership, or agency relationship between the parties.

11 · Limitation of Liability

Apollo Advancement's total liability arising out of or related to this engagement shall not exceed the total fees actually paid by Maryvale under this agreement. In no event shall Apollo Advancement be liable for any indirect, incidental, consequential, or lost revenue damages, regardless of whether such damages were foreseeable or whether Apollo Advancement was advised of the possibility of such damages.

The audit report and strategic plan represent Apollo Advancement's professional judgment and recommendations based on the information available at the time of the engagement. Apollo Advancement does not guarantee specific fundraising outcomes.

12 · Client Responsibilities

Maryvale agrees to provide timely access to documents, data, staff, and stakeholders necessary to complete the engagement. This includes, but is not limited to, donor records, financial statements, past communications, and scheduling of interviews during the on-site visit. Delays in providing requested materials may affect the timeline and scope of deliverables.

13 · Ownership of Deliverables

Upon receipt of final payment, Maryvale Academy shall own all deliverables produced under this engagement, including the written audit report and strategic development plan. Apollo Advancement retains the right to reuse its proprietary methodologies, analytical frameworks, templates, and anonymized insights in future engagements and professional development materials.

14 · Force Majeure

Neither party shall be liable for delays or failure to perform obligations under this agreement due to circumstances beyond reasonable control, including but not limited to illness, family emergency, natural disaster, or travel disruption. The affected party shall notify the other party promptly and the timeline shall be adjusted accordingly.
