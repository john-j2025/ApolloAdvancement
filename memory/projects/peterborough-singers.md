# Peterborough Singers Campaign

**Also called:** The Singers, the Syd Birrell Legacy Fund
**Status:** Active, deadline June 30, 2026
**Role:** Campaign Director
**Fee:** $30,000 (one of John's first independent consulting clients)
**Type:** $250K legacy fund honoring retiring Artistic Director Syd Birrell after 35 years

## Key Metrics
IMPORTANT: Use 80 to 325 as the approved donor figures for prospect-facing materials. Earlier project files reference 128 to 434; these are not the current approved figures.

- Campaign goal: $250,000
- Amount raised: ~$101,667
- Active donors grew from 80 to 325
- Analyzed 20 years of historical donor data
- Built a refined top-50 prospect list using composite scoring (gift capacity, donor longevity, frequency, recency, event engagement)

## Key People
- **Syd Birrell** - Retiring Artistic Director. Willing to participate in personal asks. Key asset.
- **Shawn Grenke** - New Artistic Director (announced early March 2026). Presents a strategic re-engagement opportunity.
- **Malcolm Burrows** (Scotia Wealth Management) - Strategic advisor

## Campaign Status and Challenges
- The emotional peak around Syd's retirement concerts has passed, leading to slower momentum
- Shawn Grenke's announcement is a potential re-engagement hook
- Remaining strategy emphasizes face-to-face meetings over mass communications
- Shifted strategy from mass communications to personal, face-to-face major gift solicitation

## Organizational Challenges
- Board resistance to fundraising activities
- Business manager overwhelmed by donation processing and refusing help
- General organizational discomfort with fundraising
- These map to Beliefs #1 (embarrassment), #7 (development office's job), and #9 (board is governance only)
